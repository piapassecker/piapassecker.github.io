Mara's Story

Mara is a shelter dog.
She was found on a field, where a lot of dogs were abadoned. She just lived in the shelter for a few weeks and then my dog trainer showed me a picture of her. I immediately fell in love with her and 1 week later I drove to Slovakia to take a look at her. When I arrived there she was the first of around 5 dogs who ran to me and wanted to jump into my car. It was fate! The moment I saw her in real life, I knew I had to take her with me home.
On Valentin's Day I took her to my home and since then we live together.
